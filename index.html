How To Use

The Wordle Assistor is used to help you find all potential guesses that can be used while playing Wordle. You will need to have the Wordle game open alongside the assistor to get the most out of it.

Enter your first guess into the assistor and press enter

Mimic the Wordle colors by clicking a letter to toggle the colors

Enter your next guess or click one of the guess options for your next word
